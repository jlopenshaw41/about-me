About Me

My name is Jen and I'm an [aspiring] software developer. I am part of the September 2020 Software Engineering bootcamp cohort at Manchester Codes. I am making a career-shift into tech after three years raising a family.

I have over 10 years experience in the heritage sector. I've managed historic houses open to the public, I've led teams of various shapes and sizes, I've overseen large projects and I've driven compliance across hundreds of National Trust places. Prior to that, I trained as a Graduate Analyst at Accenture IT Consultancy, spending most of my early career in the finance & banking sector. I have a history degree and a Masters in Heritage Management.

I live with my husband and our two young boys in a beautiful corner of rural Lancashire. My real passion is coding; I can't get enough of it right now, but when I am not creating things online, life takes on a somewhat slower pace. I'm usually to be found spending time with my family, enjoying the nature and wildlife around the farm where we live, or gardening or reading a book. I also love making things and in a previous life I built a small business creating and selling clothing for children.
